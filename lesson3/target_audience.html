Target Audience

Description

Ideally the first weather information you look is the one on your city. The next part of the information you will see is the weather pronostic by hour, and by day of the week. As a second goal we want to display the most recent weather events around the world, specially that are ocurring on the visitors city. We want also let people know some important news about natural dissasters and have a section to contribute to organizations that support those kind of dissasters, for example donations to the red cross. Finally, we want to display tips of how you can take of your house or yourself depending of the weather.
